Coplay supports "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK) for the latest AI models. However, advanced billing mechanics like **Context Caching** and **Token Thresholds** can drastically affect your bill. Use this guide to optimize your spending.

## üí∞ The "Hidden" Costs

Beyond the standard price per token, there are three critical rules that affect your final bill:

### 1. The 5-Minute Cache Rule
Most providers (like Anthropic and Google) offer "Context Caching" to make long chats cheaper.
* **The Trap:** Cache typically resets after **5 minutes of inactivity**.
* **The Cost:** If you wait 6 minutes to reply, the cache clears, and you are charged the **full non-cached rate** (often **10x higher**) to re-process your entire history.
* **Fix:** For long, complex sessions, try to keep your replies frequent to keep the cache alive.

### 2. The 200k Token Threshold
* **The Rule:** Once your active context (pinned files + chat history) exceeds **200,000 tokens**, many providers **double the cost per token** for the excess.
* **Fix:** If you hit this limit, immediately **Start a New Thread** to reset your token count.

### 3. The Multiplier Effect
You pay for **Input Tokens** on every single message. If you pin a 10,000-token script and ask 5 questions about it, you pay for those 10,000 tokens **5 times**.
